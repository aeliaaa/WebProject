About Roots & Canvas

Connecting Heritage, Creativity and Community

Our Mission

Roots & Canvas exists to create a digital home for Kenyan artists while preserving the stories, traditions and cultural heritage that inspire their work.

We believe art should not only be admired but also understood as a reflection of identity, history and community.

Our Vision

To become Kenya's leading digital platform for showcasing artistic talent and cultural storytelling.

Heritage

Preserving traditional artistic practices for future generations.

Artists

Providing visibility and opportunities for emerging creators.

Future

Building a bridge between cultural roots and modern creativity.

FROM TRADITION TO TOMORROW

Rooted in Heritage

Traditional art forms such as beadwork, wood carving, textile design, and storytelling continue to shape modern creative expression.

Creating the Future

Today's artists use photography, digital media, illustration, and emerging technologies to tell uniquely Kenyan stories for global audiences.
