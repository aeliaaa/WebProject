OUR ROOTS

For generations, Kenyan communities have expressed their identity through storytelling, beadwork, sculpture, painting, music, and craftsmanship.

Roots & Canvas exists to preserve these traditions while creating opportunities for modern artists to showcase their work and reach new audiences.
